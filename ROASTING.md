## Recently Closed Lots

### CGLE Mandela XO - Closed (2026-04-25)

13 batches across 4 experiment sets. Reference: **Batch 139** (v3b inlet, shaped fan 80% -> 68% -> 63% -> 70% -> 73%, drop 203.8C, Maillard 44.5%, Agtron 76).

**Roast-to-brew confirmed:** XO/heavy fermentation responds best to Balanced Intensity brewing (April Brewer, ~91C, 1:15), NOT Full Expression. Fan curve shape was the primary roast lever; dev time as an independent variable did not move the needle once RoR shape was understood.

**Aromatic fingerprint (confirmed):** caramelized pineapple, lemongrass, barbecue caramel, milk tea body, tropical fruit, light liqueur warmth at finish. Lemongrass is varietal/terroir character from CGLE (shared with Sudan Rume Natural from same farm) - reads as pungent when underdeveloped, integrated when dialed correctly.

**Failure signals:** alcoholic-dominant attack with no body or integration (underdeveloped); spice-dominant cinnamon/cardamom with muted fruit and hollow finish (overdeveloped).
