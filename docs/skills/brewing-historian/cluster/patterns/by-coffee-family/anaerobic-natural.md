# Anaerobic Natural — per-coffee-family brewing patterns

*Coffee Research · Latent · Brewing Historian cluster · by-coffee-family*

**Corpus:** N=10 brews on Natural base + Anaerobic fermentation modifier (largest coffee-family cluster in the archive as of 2026-05-26).

**Current rollup:** see [cross-coffee-insights.md § By Process > Anaerobic Natural](../cross-coffee-insights.md#by-process) for the default-strategy table entry, and [by-strategy/suppression.md](../by-strategy/suppression.md) for the per-coffee data points (three confirmed origins: Colombia / Ethiopia / Panama; process-driven pattern, not terroir-driven).

**Default strategy:** Suppression (4 data points: Inmaculada Colombia + Basha Bekele Ethiopia + Altieri NASD Panama + Picolot Simba Cold Room Ethiopia/Panama-processed).

**Key levers:**

- Temperature is the surgical lever for bitter tail; coarsening grind is the wrong move.
- Office SWORKS template: Dial 6 (Half-Open) through main pours = canonical anaerobic-natural Suppression valve template. Distinct from Dial 5 Restricted slow/slow/open (yeast-anaerobic Hybrid Sequential).
- Fallback if Suppression doesn't resolve: Balanced + Inverted Temperature Staging (88°C → 94°C across two phases). Not Balanced at higher temp.

Patterns specific to anaerobic-natural sub-types (anaerobic vs ASD/NASD vs cold-room dehydration) will accrue here as the corpus grows.
