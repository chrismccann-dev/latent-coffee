# Ethiopian landrace population — per-cultivar brewing patterns

*Coffee Research · Latent · Brewing Historian cluster · by-cultivar*

**Corpus:** N=4 brews labeled as the unspecified "Ethiopian landrace population" cultivar (distinct from 74158 / Ethiopian Landrace Blend 74110-74112 which have their own canonical entries).

## Variety default + cooling

Ethiopian landraces (74110/74112/74158 and unspecified population lots) - Clarity-First works but cups consistently improve cooling. Don't evaluate before 45-50°C.

- **Ethiopian washed landraces** - consistently peak warm-to-cool (45-50°C). Don't judge until cooled.
- **Ethiopian standard natural landraces** - same cooling pattern as washed: sharp or sequential flavors when hot integrate into a sweet, unified fruit-tea expression below 50°C. Sharp finish above 50°C is heat masking, not an extraction defect - do not iterate on the finish before the cup cools. Confirmed: Moonwake Alo Gemechu Station (Landrace, Sidama). One data point. Ethiopian standard naturals with brightness-forward flavor targets (berry, citrus, white tea) confirm Balanced Intensity lower edge (EG-1 6.4 / 94°C); for aromatic-landrace varieties (Sudan Rume, SL-lineage), watch for vehicle-driven phase separation rather than extraction depth (see [sudan-rume.md](sudan-rume.md)).

## Anaerobic washed (clean) — roaster-override case

Default Process/Variety routing calls Balanced Intensity for clean anaerobic washed; one data point (DAK Apricoast, Arbegona, Rumu Damo) confirms Full Expression when the roaster house style is Full Expression. EG-1 6.0 / 99°C kettle-on-base throughout (no taper) / Hario V60 + Cafec T-92 (LC4) / 15g / 240g (1:16) / ~4:00. Variety still expresses transparency at high EY - clean apricot/citrus/toffee, no ferment-driven funk. Citrus emerges on cooling (sub-50°C) alongside apricot and toffee; full profile evaluation requires cool window. Pattern: when clean anaerobic washed Ethiopian landrace meets a Full Expression roaster, follow the roaster. (This is a cultivar × roaster interaction - the roaster-house-style-overrides-clean-process pattern is tracked in [cross-coffee-insights.md](../cross-coffee-insights.md).)

## Per-strategy entries

- [Clarity-First](../by-strategy/clarity-first.md) — Ethiopian washed landraces (74110, 74112, 74158) from Sidama/Bensa - Clarity-First works but cups improve cooling.
- [Balanced Intensity](../by-strategy/balanced-intensity.md) — Ethiopian standard natural with brightness-forward targets (Moonwake Alo Gemechu Station, Sidama) at lower edge.
- [Full Expression](../by-strategy/full-expression.md) — Clean Ethiopian anaerobic washed (DAK Apricoast, Arbegona) when roaster house style is Full Expression.
