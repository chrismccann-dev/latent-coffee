# WBC Per-Strategy Patterns — Full Expression

**Status:** PLACEHOLDER (Wave 2, 2026-05-26) — populate with WBC competitor recipes within Latent's Full Expression strategy as patterns crystallize.

## Latent strategy framing

Full Expression = fine grind + high temperature + high agitation, deployed to develop a heavy co-ferment or natural where the cup wants to be loud. Mechanically symmetric with Extraction Push but differs in agitation discipline (Full Expression is high, Extraction Push uses Melodrip / low agitation). See [BREWING.md § Full Expression](BREWING.md).

## WBC anchors observed

- Common shape for WBC competitors pitching heavy anaerobic naturals or co-ferments — the recipe matches the coffee's projection ambitions.

## Cross-coffee patterns to author

- Document the boundary with Extraction Push (same grind + temp, different agitation).
- Identify WBC recipes where Full Expression backfired (over-extracted bitter tail) — boundary-defining failures.
