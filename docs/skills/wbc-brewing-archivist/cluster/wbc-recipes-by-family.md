# WBC Recipes by Family — Per-Competitor Archive (2022-2026)

The competitor-by-competitor recipe rows of the 154-recipe World Brewers Cup archive (2022-2026), grouped by strategy family. Split out of [wbc-recipes.md](docs/skills/wbc-brewing-archivist/cluster/wbc-recipes.md) on 2026-06-29 (Pattern J post-tripwire prune — the combined doc crossed the 60 KB single-doc cap after the 2026 drop added 52 routines). The **strategy distribution, strategically important findings, and the 65 subtype definitions** stay in the parent [wbc-recipes.md](docs/skills/wbc-brewing-archivist/cluster/wbc-recipes.md); this doc is the deep per-recipe lookup. For Latent's mapping of WBC families/subtypes onto its own 6-strategy / 3-modifier framework, see [wbc-reference.md](docs/skills/wbc-brewing-archivist/cluster/wbc-reference.md).

**This is reference material, not a canonical registry.** Use it for "what was the WBC competitor doing?" lookups during a brief, when re-framing an existing reference recipe under a new strategy classification, or when scoping a deliberate new experiment ("which 2025 finalists used Selective Bloom Hybrid?").

**Surfaced via MCP `list_docs` / `read_doc`** — claude.ai brewing sessions can pull it on demand alongside the parent.
