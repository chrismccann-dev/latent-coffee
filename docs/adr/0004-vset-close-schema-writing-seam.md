# 0004 — V-set close-out schema-vs-writing seam (per-slot cup schema with V-set-wide narrative writing)

The `experiments` table keeps its per-slot cup-side fields (`observed_outcome_a/b/c/d`, `updated_cup_prediction_a/b/c/d`, `taste_for_a/b/c/d`, `delta_from_cup_a/b/c/d`) even though lived practice produces a **V-set-wide cup narrative paragraph** + verdict instead of 18 per-slot cells. Three repair options were considered: (a) tighten practice toward the 18-cell discipline (force claude.ai to write per-slot prose for every field), (b) collapse schema to V-set-wide cup fields via migration (drop the `_a/_b/_c/_d` suffixes on cup-side cells, replace with single per-experiment narrative columns), (c) document the gap and accept it. **Option (c) wins**: pre-articulated per-slot taste-for biases tasting (single-palate honesty is non-negotiable in Chris's workflow); cup observations are inherently comparative across slots (the comparison IS the structure); lever / non-factor promotion is a lot-close artifact, not a V-set close-out artifact; roast-side observations DO decompose per-slot (~30 numeric cells per V-set, field-structured) but cup-side does not. Self-reported population rates (CGLE Sudan Rume Natural V2 close-out as case study): actual-roast 100%, roast-delta ~30% exception-write, predicted-cup ~20%, updated-cup-prediction ~10%, actual-cup 100% as V-set-wide narrative / 0% per-slot, cup-delta 100% as verdict / 0% per-slot. Schema stays as-is; writing produces the **V-set close-out narrative** (5 components: hypothesis restatement + per-slot roast metrics + per-slot roast obs + V-set-wide cup narrative + verdict-with-implication); many per-slot cup fields legitimately stay null. Future readers looking at the per-slot schema and wondering why population is so partial will find this ADR.
