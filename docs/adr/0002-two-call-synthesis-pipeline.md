# 0002 — Two-call synthesis pipeline (humanizer as separate pass)

The synthesis pipeline runs the raw directed-synthesis call (per-entity adapter + corpus + weighting + output format) and the humanizer polish call (vendored `blader/humanizer` MIT skill stripping AI-tells + preserving Latent vocabulary) as **two sequential Sonnet 4.6 calls**, not one combined prompt. The separation is intentional: the two tasks are cognitively distinct (extract-patterns-from-corpus vs rewrite-for-voice on already-drafted prose), the upstream humanizer skill is kept resync-able by isolating it from the directed prompt, and the humanizer is reused outside synthesis (LinkedIn posts, emails, other long-form writing). Accepted cost: ~2x latency (~10-15s combined) + ~2x credit spend per generation, bounded by the lazy resynthesize trigger (page-visit only, on corpus-delta only — see [CONTEXT.md § Synthesis Pipeline](../../CONTEXT.md)).
