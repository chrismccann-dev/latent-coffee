## The ratified taste-target statement (verbatim, Chris-approved)

> **The Latent taste target.** The cup is **distinctive and multilayered, and it transforms** - it moves hot-to-warm-to-cool and attack-to-body-to-aftertaste, revealing new layers as it goes. The anti-target is **one-dimensional, whether that one dimension is clean or funky**: a loud single-note fruit-bomb misses exactly as much as a flat clean cup. **Clarity is the carrier, not the goal** - clean extraction and clean processing are the price of entry, and the complexity rides on top without ever going muddy. It is **complexity made legible**.
>
> This is **opinionated and noncommercial**: the cup Chris most enjoys, deliberately *not* the clean/tea-like/high-transparency profile the broader community gravitates toward. The competition play is to brew the cup he loves and **craft the narrative argument for why it is the truest expression of coffee** - not to chase an assumed judge or crowd preference.
>
> **What produces it (primary to free):** *Process* is the deepest lever - slow, controlled, engineered naturals (dark-room/cold-room drying, dry-fermentation, oxidative, yeast-inoculated); slow-and-controlled is what lays complexity in legible layers instead of one-dimensional funk. Process-agnostic in principle, this family is the high-probability vehicle. *Terroir + producer* is a co-occurring bundle - high-altitude Panama Chiriquí and a master-producer tier (Garrido, Lamastus, Altieri, Janson) as the **starting roster, not a closed set**. *Variety* is free - exotic-distinctive genetics as a class (Gesha, Mokkita, Pacamara, Typica Mejorado), no single cultivar as the gate. *Roast* is lighter-to-medium-light - developed enough to carry layered body, never enough to bleed roast into the cup.
>
> **What it scopes:** the search space collapses from "all coffee" to high-altitude slow-engineered naturals from top-tier producers, Panama-centered but not Panama-limited. The target is what sourcing -> roasting -> brewing all resolve toward.

## Decision log (forks resolved this session)

1. **Single apex, not two targets.** The personal-love cup and the competition cup are one and the same. No separate "what I like" vs "what scores." Rejected the two-target option explicitly.
2. **Expressive-complexity over clean-transparency.** Chris's highest-order preference is the *opposite* pole from the SF/specialty consensus (ultra-light, tea-like, high-clarity). He deliberately broadened into the clean profile earlier *because it felt over-represented and not-him*; the corpus's dominant clean signal is an artifact of the brewing/optimized side, not his apex.
3. **The real variable is distinctiveness + multilayered complexity that evolves over temperature and across attack->body->aftertaste.** Not loudness, not funk. The anti-target is *one-dimensional* whether clean or funky (the "fruit blast" cup: high-process, high-fruit, but flat -> misses).
4. **Clarity is the carrier, not the goal.** Clean extraction/processing is the price of entry; complexity rides on top, never muddy. "Complexity made legible."
5. **Process is the deepest sourcing lever; variety is free; terroir+producer is a co-occurring bundle.** Decomposition of Chris's 8 named favorites: process clustered tightest (slow controlled engineered naturals - DRD/cold-room/dry-ferment/oxidative/yeast); terroir near-invariant (Panama Chiriquí x7, Ecuador x1); producer concentrated (Garrido x3, Lamastus x2, Altieri, Janson) but **confounded by PicoLot curation** - so treat the named farms as a *starting roster, not a closed set*; variety varied (Gesha 4, Mokkita 2, Pacamara, Typica Mejorado) -> not the gate, exotic-distinctive *class* is the floor. The one non-Panama favorite (Finca Soledad TyOxidator, Ecuador) is *also* a slow-engineered natural, proving process travels off the geography.
6. **Stance: noncommercial, narrative-driven, harder/higher-variance cup on purpose.** Reframe Chris added: it's not "bet judges reward what the crowd didn't" - it's "pick what I love and craft the argument for why it's the truest expression of coffee." That reframe *is* the WBC mechanism (you present an argument to judges).
