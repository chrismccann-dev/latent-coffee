## Decision ledger (the resolved model — synthesis, do not re-litigate)

**Axes & vocabulary**
1. **Three-axis spine.** **Fit** = a filter cascade + a prose scorecard sanity-check (**never a stored/computed number**) · **Role** = the five lanes · **Action** = the `bucket`. The old **§7 "Tier 1/2/3 priority targets" is RETIRED** (the bucket is its successor).
2. **Bucket lexicon.** `bucket` ∈ `pursue` / `watch` / `learning` / `calibration` / `avoid`. **`reference` → `calibration`** (rename; **zero data migration** — no producer carries `reference`, all 7 seeds are `pursue`). Lane **"Reference clarity" → "Calibration clarity."** "tier" is **retired from sourcing** — it survives ONLY as the producer-registry's system-importance field (a different axis; keep that field).
3. **Bucket meanings.** `pursue` = apex-aligned, act the moment it surfaces · `watch` = strong engineered-fit but unproven / higher-risk / no access yet (the **portability-exploration lane**) · `learning` = **broad** build-skill (new process, new variety, or roasting basics; absorbs producer-targeted value-practice — disposable commodity reps stay in the green-inventory queue, not the producer roster) · `calibration` = keep a known clean skill sharp + a fixed clean benchmark (washed Panama Gesha as the yardstick) · `avoid` = deprioritize flag, **empty of named producers today** (you don't canonically index a one-dimensional producer as a target).
4. **Types A-D are dissolved** into bucket (action) + lane (role). "Apex anchor" survives as a fit *descriptor*, not a Type. The five lanes survive as the **role axis** (Calibration-clarity / Fruit-tea-expression / Process-learning / Roast-learning-hybrids / Value-practice).

**Rules**
5. **Apex is process-defined.** Hard gate = an engineered/controlled **process signature** that yields a *layered-evolving* cup. Distinctive variety = **#2, a distinctiveness floor, not a chase** (no single variety is the gate). High altitude = strong prior. **Geography = density, not a gate** (Panama is where the lived evidence clusters; the signature is portable and actively sought elsewhere). **A variety-coverage gap routes to `learning`, not `pursue`** — apex is process-defined, never variety-defined.
6. **`pursue` is a standing recognition set**, not a single-target optimization — you pursue many at once because access is opportunistic at 1kg scale. The producer index is **pre-computed homework for a low-info, low-choice buying environment**: a bare name + hot/cool notes + a cup score (the Forward floor) is enough to act because the producer research was done in advance. **This is why there's no computed score** — you can't compute one from point-of-sale info and don't need to; the verdict is baked into the bucket. Same logic → **riskFlags stay inline in the rationale**, not a structured field, until a consumer (a card badge / avoid-filter) exists.
7. **Anti-target is structural, not a flavor vocabulary.** Boozy / funky / winey / candy descriptors are **not** disqualifiers — Chris likes them and is non-commercial. The cutoff fires only on **one-dimensional + loud + no evolution across the cooling/structural arc**. (Worked exemplars: *Candy Blast* — co-ferment watermelon, one static candy note → fails; *Project One Light* — same yeast-anaerobic family, but pu'er→peach-candy→jasmine-milk-tea evolving → apex.) **Inject-engineering is admitted when it lands legible** (the keystone).
8. **New tells** (fold into § 0): **temperature-swing test** (score the divergence between hot/cool stations, not the note-list prettiness — *score the swing, not the vocabulary*, on the risk side too), **producer-signature-vs-lot-buy** (the producer opens the door; the specific lot decides which/how-many), **named-exemplar** ("more like the HiU Los Lajones") + **range-over-depth** (breadth of single-1kg range-finders over depth on one lot while the apex direction is still being learned).

**Scorecard reweight** (§ 9, prose sanity-frame only): Process signature 25 · Layered-evolving output read 20 · **Personal taste evidence 20** (was 5 — it's the *origin* of the target, not a tiebreaker) · Producer track record 15 · Cultivar fit 12 · Terroir/elevation/density 8. Drying quality **removed** (folded into Process signature — a 1kg buyer never gets drying detail). **Missing data is neutral, not negative.** The real operational form is the **filter cascade** (hard process gate → temperature-swing read → producer signal → portfolio gap → not-classic-clean → dedup-vs-inventory); the scorecard is a back-check.
